# Project Governance - BiotechProject 🧬

## 1. Philosophy: The "Father of the Project" Model
BiotechProject follows a **Benevolent Dictator For Life (BDFL)** governance model, inspired by the leadership structures of foundational open-source projects like the Linux Kernel.

* [cite_start]**Lead Maintainer:** Fabrizio (@Gitechnolo) retains ultimate authority over the project's technical direction, core architecture, and final approval of all Pull Requests[cite: 90, 92].
* [cite_start]**Core Principles:** No contribution or external influence shall compromise the **Zero-Framework Mandate (ADR-001)** or the **Privacy-by-Architecture** (100% client-side data processing)[cite: 7, 11, 65, 70].

## 2. Decision Making & ADR Process
[cite_start]Technical evolution is strictly managed through **Architectural Decision Records (ADR)** to ensure SRE-grade reliability[cite: 84].
* Any major change or feature request must be proposed as an Issue for architectural review.
* [cite_start]The Lead Maintainer evaluates proposals based on their impact on the **Global Resilience Score** and technical maturity[cite: 17, 23, 66].
* Decisions are documented as ADRs to ensure long-term stability and transparency, mirroring the rigor of existing records (ADR-001 to ADR-005).

## 3. Roles and Responsibilities
* [cite_start]**Lead Maintainer (The "Father"):** Defines the long-term vision, manages the security of the "Stateless Edge," and acts as the final arbiter for the **Metabolic Digital Twin Architecture**[cite: 10, 76, 83].
* [cite_start]**Maintainers:** Trusted contributors appointed by the Lead Maintainer to oversee specific modules, such as i18n expansion or WHO Data Integration[cite: 38, 45].
* **Contributors:** Community members providing code, scientific content, or translations. Contributions are meritocratic and must pass the **AI-Assisted SRE Auditing (ADR-003)** protocol.

## 4. Financial & Institutional Neutrality
BiotechProject may accept grants or institutional hosting (e.g., from global open-source foundations) to scale its impact, but it maintains strict technical independence.
* [cite_start]**Grant Usage:** External funds are strictly allocated to project milestones, such as **Offline Sync Engine** development, **WCAG AAA** audits, and **Replicability Toolkits**[cite: 36, 40, 44, 52].
* [cite_start]**Autonomy:** Institutional support or financial contributions do not grant decision-making power over the core architecture or the "Zero-Framework" philosophy[cite: 65, 83].

## 5. Open Source Guarantee
BiotechProject is and will always remain open source.
* [cite_start]**Source Code:** Distributed under the **MIT License**[cite: 63].
* **Content & Documentation:** Distributed under **CC BY 4.0**.
* [cite_start]Any attempt to enforce proprietary "lock-in" or vendor dependency is strictly prohibited by this charter[cite: 65, 85].
