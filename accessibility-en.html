Biotech Project Accessibility Statement

BiotechProject is built on the principle of Equity-by-Design. We believe that health innovation and scientific data must be resilient and accessible to everyone, regardless of sensory, cognitive, or motor abilities.

This platform is committed to digital inclusion and strictly complies with international standards, in line with the Legislative Decree 10 August 2018, n. 106 (transposing Directive (EU) 2016/2102) and the latest WCAG updates.

🥇 Conformance Status

Fully Conformingto the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 - Level AA, with significant implementations of Level AAAcriteria.

  • Last Technical Audit:March 06, 2026
  • Aggregate Accessibility Score:100/100 (Lighthouse SRE Audit)
  • Methodology:Multi-model SRE auditing (Gemini 1.5 Pro & Copilot), automated axe-core engines, and manual testing with NVDA/VoiceOver.

🚀 Strategic Vision for Level AAA

We treat performance and accessibility as non-negotiable clinical requirements. Our roadmap includes:

  • Universal Language Bridge:Expanding our modular i18n system to support automated plain-language (Easy-to-Read) versions for all scientific modules.
  • SRE-for-Humans:Maintaining a 95% aggregate performance score even under 4x CPU slowdown to prevent session timeouts for motor-impaired users.
  • Enhanced Semantics:Full adoption of ARIA 1.2 for all metabolic digital twin simulations.
