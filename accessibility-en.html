Biotech Project Accessibility Statement

Biotech Project is built on the strong belief that scientific knowledge must be universal. Accessibility is not just a technical requirement, but a core principle of our design and communication.

This website is committed to digital accessibility and fully complies with international standards, in line with the Legislative Decree 10 August 2018, n. 106 (transposing Directive (EU) 2016/2102).

ü•á Conformance Status

Fully Conforming to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 - Level AA.

  • Last Technical Review:November 8, 2025
  • Methodology:Self-assessment based on automated tools (Lighthouse, axe) and manual testing with screen readers (NVDA, VoiceOver) and keyboard navigation.

üöÄ Our Commitment to Level AAA

Biotech Project continues to evolve, aiming to exceed Level AA and reach Level AAA in non-mandatory areas. Our current focus is on:

  • Pronunciation Guide:Expanding Text-to-Speech functionality to include assisted pronunciation for all acronyms and complex terms.
  • Full Transcriptions:Integration of complete transcriptions for all future video and audio elements.
  • Continuous Improvement:Ongoing monitoring of the readability of non-simplified content.
